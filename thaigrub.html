Kaeng Khiaowan Kai

Green curry is a central Thai variety of curry, based on coconut milk and fresh green chillies. The green chillies lend this heavenly green curry chicken & roti dish its signature green color.

Green Papaya Salad

This signature dish of Southeast Asia is made from unripe shredded papaya, beans, chili, lime, dressed in savory fish sauce.

Floating Market

Try this for grocery shopping: head to one of Bangkok's floating markets and get local fruits, produce, arts, & crafts, even DISHES (yes, dishes prepared in FLOATING KITCHENS) from boats traversing up and down mini canals.

Tom Kha Gai

Spicy & sour chicken coconut soup made from coconut milk, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, Thai chili peppers, coriander, straw mushrooms.

Street Market

Grab a bite of authentic, cheap Thai grub at one of its street food markets--day or night, rain or shine.
