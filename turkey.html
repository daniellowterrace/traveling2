TURKEY

Turkey is a deeply modern, ancient country that warrants repeated visits. Whether you're interested in avant-garde and ancient art and culture, or progressive politics and poetry; enjoy drinking tea and eating fresh, local and delicious food; or searching for one's Armenian heritage, and looking for the remnants of a diminished civilization... One of the joys of exploring Turkey is discovering the unpredictable and at times eccentric architecture... In fact, the visual structure and "design" of this webpage (with too many photos) is a reference to the architecture that one might find in Turkey. Starting with the sky, then the round windows, between "floors" and at the bottom is a door or two...

Below, arranged alphabetically, are some of our favorite places to go, things to see and people to meet... All reasons to visit Turkey...

Architecture

In Turkey one finds fascinating architecture: from the troglodytes to today's inhabitants, from caves to ancient structures to postmodern residences; sometimes seemingly eccentric and thoroughly original, there's always something to look at and something to learn...

Armenia

In 1915... The remnants and remains of a vibrant civilization can be found in more places than one might imagine...

Art

Like many places, art is everywhere... From Armenian to Byzantine, from Orientalist ornamentation to Post-Postmodernism; patterns are omnipresent there's a lot to see...

Food
