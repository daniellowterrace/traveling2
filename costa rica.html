Cool-looking plant

So many amazing plants in Costa Rica.

Chocolate plant pod

I didn't realize a chocolate plant pod was so large. It takes 10 of these to make a regular-sized bar of chocolate.

Freshly ground chocolate

Fresh from a plant in the farm. Made for a perfect hot chocolate. I mixed mine with pepper, salt, and vanilla - delicious!

Rambutan Fruit

I always wonder how people discover fruits. Looking at the outside of this, I would never imagine the inside would be a tasty, lychee-like fruit.

Volcano

There are a lot of volcanos to see in Costa Rica. During rainy season, with the cloud cover, you may not realize how close you are to one.

Poor Man's Umbrella Plant

Leaves so huge; you can see where it gets it's knickname!
